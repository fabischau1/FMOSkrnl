Commands

Activate Terminal:
FMOSterminal.Terminal()

Run js in terminal:
FMOSterminal.runNeonTerminalJS()

fullscreen:
FMOSterminal.toggleTerminalFullscreen()

Close Terminal:
FMOSterminal.HideTerminal()
